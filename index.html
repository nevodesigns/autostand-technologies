The Innovation

Automated Production Machine

A semi-automatic manufacturing system that transforms raw materials into finished phone stands with minimal human intervention.

01

Material Feeding

Raw plastic sheets or molded components are introduced into the machine.

02

Detection & Alignment

Sensors detect material position and ensure precise alignment before processing.

03

Processing Stage

Cutting, shaping, drilling, and finishing operations are performed automatically.

04

Assembly

Individual components are assembled automatically or semi-automatically.

05

Quality Inspection

Sensors verify dimensional accuracy and product consistency at each unit.

06

Product Collection

Completed phone stands are deposited into a collection tray for packaging.
